A little bit about me.......
I was born in Aberdeen Maryland and I am a military brat, moving every two years until I moved to Florida in 1996. I have lived all over the country, including Virginia, Michigan, and Mississippi. Outside of the US, I have lived in Germany three times, once in Heidelberg, the second time in Osthofen and the third in Schwabisch Hall. Being very young, I picked up the language quickly and my parents would use me as their interpreter. Today, I can only speak a few words. I was married in 2002 and we had two children, Max and Morgan. They are my everything. My husband has since passed away so I am now a single mom. My mother now lives with us and she helps me tremendously. Her help allowed me to pursue a Masters degree in Forensic Behavioral Analysis. I didn't realize I would have so much difficulty finding a job with that degree but I eventually was hired as a probation and parole officer for the state of Missouri.
